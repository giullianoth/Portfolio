How my methodology brings projects to life.

Discovery & UX Research

Immersion and Analysis: Understanding user needs and technical requirements. Defining project objectives and mapping out the functionalities critical to the application's success.

Architecture & Design

Wireframing and UI: Creation of information architecture and high-fidelity prototyping. I plan the interface focusing on usability, accessibility, and visual consistency (Design System).

Development

Code and Performance: Implementation using modern technologies such as React and Node.js. Focus on clean code, componentization, typing with TypeScript, and performance optimization.

QA & Tests

Technical Validation: Performing functionality, responsiveness, and accessibility tests. I ensure the application is robust across different devices and browsers.

Deploy & Delivery

Strategic Launch: Environment setup and application deployment. I deliver not just the code, but a solution ready to scale and generate immediate value.
